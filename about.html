Our Foundation

Rooted in Community.
Built for the Healers.

Body Bend Yoga Community Foundation was born from a simple truth: the people who teach healing often cannot afford to be healed themselves. We are here to change that.

Mission

What We Do

Body Bend Yoga Community Foundation is committed to making holistic wellness accessible for all. We provide free and low-cost yoga services to underserved communities, offer inclusive corporate wellness programs, and fund scholarships for wellness educators so they can continue deepening their knowledge and expanding their practice.

We address the growing need for healing and self-regulation by equipping individuals with empowering tools through yoga, mindfulness, and community care.

Vision

Where We Are Going

A world where every wellness educator has the financial stability to dedicate their life to their practice, and every community has access to the healing that follows. We envision a nationwide network of empowered teachers — awarding 100 to 400 scholarships annually — each one multiplying our reach into communities that need it most.

This is not just a foundation. It is a movement toward spiritual, physical, and economic wellness for all.

What We Stand For

Our Story

Why This Foundation Exists

The wellness industry is booming — and yet the teachers who make it possible are among the least financially supported professionals in the country. Trainings cost $3,000 to $10,000. Class pay ranges from $25 to $75. The math does not add up.

Body Bend Yoga Community Foundation was created to bridge that gap. Founded by wellness educators who lived this reality, BBYCF exists so that teachers never have to choose between deepening their practice and paying their bills.

We started in Southern California — hosting community classes, workshops, and wellness events — and we are building toward a national network of funded, empowered educators who carry healing into every corner of this country.

Leadership

Drew Muse

President (CEO)

Drew Muse is a wellness educator and community builder whose vision drives Body Bend Yoga Community Foundation. Having experienced firsthand the financial barriers that prevent teachers from deepening their practice, Drew created BBYCF to ensure that no educator has to choose between growth and stability.

Under Drew's leadership, the foundation is building a nationwide network of empowered wellness practitioners equipped to serve their communities with knowledge, confidence, and care.
