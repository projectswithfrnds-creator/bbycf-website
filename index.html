Our Foundation

We Invest in the Healers

The wellness industry is one of the fastest-growing sectors in America, yet the teachers who lead it are among the least financially supported. Trainings cost $3,000–$10,000. Class pay ranges from $25–$75. The math doesn't work.

Body Bend Yoga Foundation exists to close that gap. We provide scholarships to yoga, meditation, sound healing, breathwork, herbalism, and reiki practitioners so they can deepen their knowledge, stay current in their field, and serve their communities from a place of stability and confidence.

When a teacher grows, an entire community heals. That's the return on this investment.

What We Do

Programs & Services

From scholarships to community events, every program is designed to expand access and empower practitioners at every level.

🎓

Teacher Training Scholarships

We fund continuing education for wellness educators, covering yoga certifications, sound healing trainings, meditation retreats, breathwork courses, and more. No teacher should have to choose between growth and stability.

🌿

Community Wellness Workshops

Low-cost and donation-based yoga, meditation, and wellness events open to all, with proceeds reinvested directly into scholarships and programming. Community care in action.

🏢

Corporate Wellness Programs

Tailored wellness sessions for organizations and their teams. Every corporate partnership directly funds access for underserved communities and scholarship recipients.

♿

Accessible Yoga Services

Private, adaptive yoga sessions for individuals with disabilities, ensuring that wellness is truly available to every body, regardless of ability or income.

🔬

Wellness Research

We partner with graduate researchers to study the long-term effects of yoga and mindfulness on community health, publishing findings that validate and advance the field.

🌍

National Expansion Network

Beginning in Orange County and Los Angeles, we are building a national network of scholarship alumni who host workshops, trainings, and retreats in their own communities.
