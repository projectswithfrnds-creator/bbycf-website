Our Foundation

We Invest
in the Healers

The wellness industry is one of the fastest-growing sectors in America, yet the teachers who lead it are among the least financially supported. Trainings cost $3,000 to $10,000. Class pay ranges from $25 to $75. The math does not work.

Body Bend Yoga Community Foundation exists to close that gap — providing scholarships to yoga, meditation, sound healing, breathwork, herbalism, and reiki practitioners so they can deepen their knowledge and serve their communities from a place of stability and confidence.

When a teacher grows, an entire community heals.

What We Do

Programs & Services

From scholarships to community events, every program is designed to expand access and empower practitioners at every level of their journey.

01

Teacher Training Scholarships

Funding continuing education for wellness educators covering yoga, sound healing, meditation, breathwork, and more.

02

Community Wellness Workshops

Low-cost and donation-based wellness events open to all, with proceeds reinvested directly into scholarships.

03

Corporate Wellness Programs

Tailored wellness sessions for organizations and their teams. Every partnership directly funds community access.

04

Accessible Yoga Services

Private, adaptive yoga sessions for individuals with disabilities, ensuring wellness is available to every body.

05

Wellness Research

Partnering with graduate researchers to study the long-term effects of yoga and mindfulness on community health.

06

National Expansion Network

Building a national network of scholarship alumni who host workshops and retreats in their own communities.

Founder & President
Leadership

Drew Muse

President (CEO) & Treasurer

Drew Muse is a wellness educator and community builder whose vision drives Body Bend Yoga Community Foundation. Having experienced firsthand the financial barriers that prevent teachers from deepening their practice, Drew created BBYCF to ensure that no educator has to choose between growth and stability.

Under Drew's leadership, the foundation is building a nationwide network of empowered wellness practitioners equipped to serve their communities with knowledge, confidence, and care.
