Your Impact

What Your
Donation Does

Every contribution to Body Bend Yoga Community Foundation is reinvested directly into the community. We fund teacher scholarships, support free wellness events, and build the infrastructure for a nationwide healing network.

$25 Covers the cost of a community wellness class for someone who cannot afford it
$100 Contributes toward a teacher's workshop or continuing education course
$500 Funds a partial scholarship for a yoga teacher training program
$1,000 Covers a full scholarship for a wellness educator to advance their certification
$5,000 Sponsors a community wellness event serving 50 to 100 attendees
